cal poly pomona art department

brand identity

about

client / the art department at cal poly pomona
role / concept, design, illustration

The goal of this assignment was to create a new brand identity for Cal Poly Pomona's Art Department. This included a custom-designed modular font, a new logotype, a brand style guide, an event poster, and environmental graphics.

research

Part of the assignment was to create a custom typeface and logo to represent the department. The following typeface was inspired by the building that harbors the art department and the iconic CLA building which resides nearby.

style guide

Included in project are environmental graphics that could be integrated in the building.
